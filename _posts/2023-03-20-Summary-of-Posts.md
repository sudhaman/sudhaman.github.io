AI Timeline and Milestones <a name="AITimeline" />
-------------------------------------------------

### Historical Eras
1. **1940s-1950s**: Theoretical foundations (Turing, von Neumann)
2. **1960s-1970s**: Early enthusiasm and first AI winter
3. **1980s-1990s**: Expert systems renaissance
4. **2000s-2010s**: Big data revolution and deep learning
5. **2020s-Present**: Generative AI breakthrough era

### Key Milestones
- **1950**: Turing Test introduced
- **1956**: Dartmouth Conference - AI term coined
- **1997**: IBM Deep Blue defeats chess champion
- **2016**: AlphaGo wins against Go champion
- **2022**: ChatGPT launches to mainstream
- **2023-2025**: Multimodal AI systems emerge
